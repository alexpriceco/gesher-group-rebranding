Meet Gesher Group

Gesher Group is a student consulting organization that aims to bridge the gap between students of UC Santa Cruz and the professional world. Through a combination of educational workshops, guest speakers and consulting work with our valued clients, members of Gesher Group are able to develop and implement professional skills prior to entering the workforce. With the knowledge and experience gained, our members have a running start after graduation.

Gesher Culture

“Gesher” is Hebrew for the word bridge. Here at Gesher Group, our members are expected to embody the core values B.R.I.D.G.E.

Build your knowledge and skill set
Recognize the challenges ahead and bias toward action
Inspire others around you to succeed
Diversity matters. Celebrate distinct outlooks and backgrounds
Grow your perspective and ethics
Educate yourself and pass your knowledge on to others
