Target Case Study Competition

Registration due by Sunday January 29th

Gesher Group would like to present our first case study competition sponsored by Target! Teams of 5 or less will have the opportunity to win $1,000 (1st place), $750 (2nd place), and $200 (3rd place) for presenting the best solution of a case study to a panel of Target representatives. Our kickoff event will be Tuesday January 31st where you and your team will receive more information on the competition, rules, and expectations.

Location is TBD and will be sent out via email as the 31st approaches.

See you all there!
